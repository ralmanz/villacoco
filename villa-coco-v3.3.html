Surf La Punta

The most consistent wave in Panama — ranked among the best in Central America. Five minutes from Villa Coco. Boards and lessons for all levels.

Dive Coiba

A UNESCO World Heritage Site. Whale sharks, hammerheads, dolphins, turtles and coral reefs in crystal Pacific waters. Day trips arranged from the hotel.

Island Hopping

Explore Cebaco and the surrounding archipelago. Pristine volcanic beaches, deserted bays, turquoise water — almost no one else around.

Yoga & Surf Retreats

Six-night all-inclusive retreats with Waluaa Surf & Yoga. Surf coaching, yoga, meditation — relaxed, welcoming, with a pampered touch.

Horseback Riding

Ride through jungle and along volcanic black-sand beaches — one of the most memorable ways to experience Panama's Pacific coast.
